About Me

Ko Ali Harrison toku ingoa, He uri tenei nō Ngai Tahu, Tuhourangi, Ngati Whakaue, Ngā Puhi, Waikato, Mōkai me Ngati Ingarangi, Ngati Wiwi hoki. I tipu ake ahau ki Mōtupohue. E noho ana ahau inaianei ki Otautahi. Hi, I'm Ali , I come from Bluff but have just relocated to Christchurch. I am 19 years young and keen to continue my learning journey. My values and identity are pretty strong, I was raised in and by a village both in Bluff and Rotorua (Whakarewarewa). This allowed me to grow up with strong beliefs and cultural values. I know where I belong, who supports me and where I stand in my wider whanau.
